# SelectHealth AI/BI Workshop — Agenda

**Audience:** Carla Lange + 2 senior analysts (one query-focused, one Tableau-focused)
**Format:** Hands-on, light on theory. Everyone in the workspace doing the clicks.
**Goal:** See first-hand what AI/BI on Databricks can do for the kind of querying, dashboards, and reports the team builds today in Tableau — and assess whether it's a meaningful fit.

> **Data:** We'll work from a shared synthetic medical dataset (encounters with ICD-10 diagnoses, procedures, providers, facilities, and outcomes like readmissions, mortality, and complications). No PHI, so everyone can build freely. It's structured to mirror the provider- and facility-level analysis the team does, so the skills transfer directly to your real SelectHealth data.

## What attendees need
- Access to the workshop Databricks workspace (accounts + permissions provisioned ahead of time).
- That's it — no prep required. Bring curiosity and, optionally, a question or report you'd like to recreate.

## A note on flexibility
This agenda is a guide, not a script. If a topic sparks more interest, we'll lean in; if something lands quickly, we'll move on. The point is for the team to get hands-on and form a real opinion.
